# Subsets — Research & Foundations

Enumerating all subsets — systematic backtracking (include/exclude each element), walking the search tree.

- **S. W. Golomb & L. D. Baumert (1965), “Backtrack programming,”** *Journal of the ACM* 12(4):516–524. The paper that formalised **backtracking** — build partial solutions and abandon a branch as soon as it cannot lead to a valid completion.

**Why it matters here:** Backtracking (Golomb–Baumert, 1965) is exactly this: extend partial solutions and abandon branches that cannot complete.
