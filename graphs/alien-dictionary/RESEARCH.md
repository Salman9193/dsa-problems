# Alien Dictionary — Research & Foundations

Recovering a total order from pairwise ordering constraints — a topological sort over a graph built from adjacent-word comparisons.

- **A. B. Kahn (1962), “Topological sorting of large networks,”** *Communications of the ACM* 5(11):558–562. The in-degree / queue topological-sort algorithm.

**Why it matters here:** Each adjacent pair of words yields one ‘letter before letter’ edge; a topological sort of those edges reconstructs the alphabet (or reports a contradiction).
