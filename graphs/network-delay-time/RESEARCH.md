# Network Delay Time — Research & Foundations

Single-source shortest paths in a weighted graph with non-negative edges — the textbook use of Dijkstra’s algorithm.

- **E. W. Dijkstra (1959), “A note on two problems in connexion with graphs,”** *Numerische Mathematik* 1:269–271. DOI: 10.1007/BF01386390. Introduces the shortest-path algorithm (Problem 2) — and, in the same paper, an MST algorithm (Problem 1). https://doi.org/10.1007/BF01386390

**Why it matters here:** The signal-propagation time is the max shortest-path distance from the source; Dijkstra computes exactly those distances.
