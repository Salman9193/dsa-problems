# Accounts Merge — Research & Foundations

Merging records that share any common key — connected components over an ‘accounts share an email’ graph, a textbook union-find application.

- **R. E. Tarjan (1975), “Efficiency of a good but not linear set union algorithm,”** *Journal of the ACM* 22(2):215–225. DOI: 10.1145/321879.321884. Union–find with union by rank and path compression — near-linear (inverse-Ackermann) disjoint-set operations. https://doi.org/10.1145/321879.321884

**Why it matters here:** Two accounts belong together iff they are transitively linked by shared emails; union-find groups them into components efficiently.
